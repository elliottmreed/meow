# Shared Rules and Instructions for All Prompts

## General Guidelines
- Ensure clarity and conciseness in all prompts.
- Avoid ambiguous language to minimize confusion.
- Use examples where applicable to illustrate complex topics.

## Formatting Rules
- Use markdown for formatting where necessary.
- Headers should be used to break content into digestible sections.
- Code snippets should be encapsulated within backticks.

## Response Expectations
- Responses should be informative and directly answer the prompts.
- Maintain a neutral tone unless instructed otherwise.
- Verify information before responding to avoid the spread of misinformation.

## Review Process
- Prompts should undergo a validation review before final acceptance.
- Feedback from peers should be incorporated into final revisions.

## Updates and Revisions
- Review prompts periodically to ensure they remain relevant and accurate.
- Clearly document changes made to prompts in your commit message.
